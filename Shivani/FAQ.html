FAQ's

1. How many lists can I make at once?

At most 5 lists can be made at once (i.e. if you have 5 lists you will need to remove at least one list to add a new one).

2. Do I have to complete a task or can I remove it, if it’s not required anymore?

You can remove the task if you do not want to do it.

3. Do I have to create an account to use ProPlanner?

Yes, you must have an account to use ProPlanner. This allows you to have cool functionalities such as sharing.

4. How do I let the system know that I have completed a task

You have the option to ‘tick’ a task once it is completed. You'll also earn points for the tasks you complete. The more the better!

5. Can I delete a whole list?

Yes, you can delete a whole list. This will remove all the tasks within it.
